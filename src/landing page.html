✦ Features

Everything you need
to ace the semester

From managing daily tasks to AI-powered quiz preparation — CampusMate covers your entire academic workflow.

📋
Task Manager
Track current, past, and overdue assignments. Never miss a deadline with due-date reminders and class-based organization.
🤖
AI Quiz Generator
Type any topic and get a fully generated quiz in seconds. Choose the number of items, type, and difficulty — perfect for exam prep.
📅
Smart Calendar
View your schedule by month, week, or day. Philippine public holidays are automatically marked so you never miss a break.
📝
Exam Tracker
Log incoming and past exams, enter results, and review your performance over time. Stay on top of your academic standing.
📊
Dashboard Overview
See all your pending, overdue, and completed activities at a glance. Your personalized dashboard keeps you focused daily.
🔔
Class Organization
Group tasks and exams by subject. Add new classes, set colors, and keep each course's work neatly separated.
